# ADR-097: Publish one falsifiable model thesis without making narrative normative

- Status: Accepted as public explanatory entrypoint; no normative wire change
- Date: 2026-07-19
- Chinese mirror: [`ADR-097-publish-one-falsifiable-model-thesis-without-making-narrative-normative.zh-CN.md`](ADR-097-publish-one-falsifiable-model-thesis-without-making-narrative-normative.zh-CN.md)

## Context

The executable model has a normal form, but public understanding still requires navigating a long README, dozens of Profiles and many ADRs. A new consensus needs one compact claim that connects household continuity, Agent-readable knowledge, 3D representations, people/pets/time, renovation and action accountability. Narrative compression can also become a dangerous hidden specification if not subordinated to exact normative sources.

## Decision

1. Publish one bilingual Model Thesis as the primary explanatory entrypoint.
2. State the central claim as household-controlled portable knowledge history independent of one Agent, platform or device graph.
3. Explain the conceptual kernel, three behavioral contracts, package binding, seven axioms, eight extension slots and observe–resolve–act–feedback loop in household language.
4. Connect 3D representations, installed influence, people/pets/time, anticipation, renovation and procurement without promoting them into Core truth or authority.
5. Include explicit falsification and evidence-limit sections. External disagreement remains retained evidence, not a score.
6. Give the thesis no normative authority. Exact Core, Profile and Conformance Set documents always win; contradiction requires correcting the thesis, not interpreting the schema through rhetoric.
7. Validate bilingual presence of the model boundary and normative firewall.

## Consequences

- A new reader can evaluate the project's wager before reading implementation detail.
- The public narrative is now derived from the tested normal form rather than accumulated slogans.
- Future communication can quote one stable thesis while still linking every normative claim to exact sources.
- Narrative popularity cannot change wire semantics, adoption status or consensus evidence.
